Chippy's Angels

Who Are We?

We're a tight-knit community dedicated to keeping people and their beloved pets together, ensuring their happiness and well-being. Our commitment extends beyond just words; we're volunteers, driven by compassion, who believe in a world where no one should ever face separation from their furry companions due to financial constraints. We operate entirely without charging any fees, relying solely on the goodwill of our volunteers and supporters.

Our mission is to offer a helping hand to those who might otherwise endure their hardships in silence. Please don't hesitate to reach out to us and share your storiesâ€”we genuinely care about your well-being!

What We Do

We understand that life can bring unexpected challenges, and we're here to provide support when it's needed most. Our belief is rooted in the value of people and their pets, recognizing that everyone deserves the love and companionship of a furry friend.

We are here to assist individuals and families who are truly in difficult circumstances, ensuring their well-being and that of their beloved pets. If you're going through a tough time, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're more than happy to help you and your family, including your furry companions.

Our Angels

At our charity, we're all about fostering the special bonds between pets and their families. While our primary goal is to ensure pets can stay with their loving families, we understand that, in some cases, rehoming becomes a necessary and compassionate solution. In such instances, we take great care to ensure a safe and caring transition for our beloved furry friends. Our unwavering commitment is to the well-being of both pets and people.
